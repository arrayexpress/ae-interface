Have comments or questions on a specific ArrayExpress page?

Please use the "Feedback" button at the top right hand corner of every ArrayExpress page. On the feedback form, please describe what you're trying to do on the page, what went wrong or what you noticed to be odd/incorrect. Please leave your name and email address if you would like a response.

 

Submission queries? Still lost after reading help pages?

Drop us an email. Please include information such as the URL of the page you were on, any error messages you've seen, or a screenshot of the page where you had a problem.

General questions: arrayexpress@ebi.ac.uk.

Annotare webform tool submitters: annotare@ebi.ac.uk.

We welcome enquiries on data submission and access, as well as more technical issues, e.g. ArrayExpress database infrastructure. A curator will usually respond to your email within 3-5 working days.

 

We're on Twitter!

Follow us at ArrayExpressEBI and the #AnnotareEBI hashtag for submission-related news.

 

RSS feeds for ArrayExpress experiment public releases

We provide an RSS service listing experiments as they become public in the ArrayExpress Archive so you can be aware of new experiments that may be of interest to you. The URL for the RSS feed is http://www.ebi.ac.uk/arrayexpress/rss/v2/experiments.
